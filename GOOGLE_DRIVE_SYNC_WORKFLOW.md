# Google Drive PDF Synchronization Workflow

This document outlines the step-by-step process for creating a group and synchronizing PDF files from a user's Google Drive into it.

## Step 1: Create an Empty Group

1.  **Navigate to "My Library"**: From the main screen, ensure you are on the "My Library" tab.
2.  **Initiate Group Creation**: Tap the **+** Floating Action Button at the bottom of the screen.
3.  **Name the Group**: You will be taken to the "Create Group" screen. Enter a descriptive name for your new group in the text field.
4.  **Confirm Creation**: Tap the "Create Group" button. The app will create a new, empty group and return you to the "My Library" screen.

## Step 2: Sync Files from Google Drive

1.  **Open Group Details**: On the "My Library" screen, tap on the newly created group. This will take you to the group's detail screen, which will initially show the message "This group is empty."
2.  **Initiate Sync**: Tap the **+** Floating Action Button (which now represents "Sync with Google Drive"). This action will navigate you to the Google Drive integration screen.
3.  **Sign In and Authorize**:
    *   If you are not already signed in, tap the "Sign in to Google Drive" button.
    *   A Google Sign-In prompt will appear. Choose the desired Google account.
    *   Grant the app permission to **view files in your Google Drive**. This permission is read-only and is required to list your PDF files.
4.  **Select Files**:
    *   Once signed in, the app will display a list of all PDF files found in your Google Drive.
    *   Use the checkboxes to select the files you wish to download and add to your group.
5.  **Download and Import**:
    *   After selecting your files, tap the "Download Selected Files" button.
    *   The app will begin downloading the files in the background. The files are saved securely in the app's private internal storage, meaning they cannot be accessed by other apps.
6.  **Return to Group**:
    *   Once the download and import process is complete, you will be automatically navigated back to the group detail screen.
    *   The screen will now display the list of PDF files you just imported from Google Drive. You can tap on any file to start reading.
